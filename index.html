Visit Rome The Eternal City

Rome, the capital of Italy is a city with lots of importance in history and culture in Europe. It was once the center of the ancient Roman empire whos power and influence has left it's mark today which can be seen all throughout the city of Rome. During ancient times Rome it was called the "Eternal City" as it was believed that it would always remain standing no matter what happpened to the rest of the world.

Fun Facts

  • Rome has over 280 fountains and over 900 churches
  • Nearly 700,000 euros worth of coins are tossed into Rome's Trevi Fountain each year.

Things to do

Take a tour of the expansive Roman Colosseum

Tour the Vatican museum, Sistine chapel or St. Peter's Basilica
